WHY SOUTHWEST FORKLIFT?

SouthWest Forklift helps qualified forklift drivers find and get jobs after they complete OSHA Certification. We provide the best forklift training and thats is why we are the # 1 Forklift Training Facility to get certified in the Houston and Dallas areas. Our forklift training provide actual hands-on experience with the actual forklifts that are on-site. Our training includes testing lectures of forklift safety, safety video, testing exam, hands-on training on the actual forklifts and performance evaluations. When safety is at stake there is no room for compromise, so we strive to maintain the highest standard of accuracy, quality and professionalism. Forklift training is our business. Let us help you with your training, so you can perform your best work. We guarantee and promise to give individuals and commercial businesses the best forklift training.
